Background

The work behind the tools.

I've spent my career at the intersection of sales strategy and operational execution — turning messy CRM data, territory files, and call activity logs into clear pictures that help leaders make faster, better decisions.

The tools in this portfolio were built out of real frustration: analysis that should take an hour was taking days, and insights were living in spreadsheets no one could actually use in a meeting. I started building my own.

I bring the same rigor to process design, quota modeling, and incentive comp — with enough technical depth to bridge the gap between what the data says and what the business needs to hear.
